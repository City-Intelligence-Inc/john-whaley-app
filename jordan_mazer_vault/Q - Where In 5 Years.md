# Q: Where Do You See Yourself In 5 Years?

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s advice from [[Interview Tips]].

## His Take
> "This is a loyalty test, not a career planning session."

## How to Answer
- Talk about sharpening your craft
- Talk about expanding your impact
- **"Wherever I can best serve the business"** — lands every time

## What to Avoid
- Specific titles or roles
- Anything that implies you'll leave
- Anything that sounds like you're using them as a stepping stone
