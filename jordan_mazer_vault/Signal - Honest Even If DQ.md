# Signal 2: Honest Even If Self-Disqualifying

Part of [[5 Candidate Signals]] by [[Jordan Mazer]].

## What It Means
Great candidates don't want to win a job they aren't a fit for.
They share information even if it might end the process.

## Why This Works Counterintuitively
> Being willing to disqualify yourself actually makes you MORE likely to get hired.
> It signals integrity and confidence — rare traits.

## How to Demonstrate It
If something about the role doesn't fit well, say it. Frame it as wanting to find a real match, not just win.
