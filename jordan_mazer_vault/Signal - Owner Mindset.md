# Signal 5: Owner Mindset

Part of [[5 Candidate Signals]] by [[Jordan Mazer]].

## What It Means
When asked why you want a job:
- ❌ Some lead with what they will **take** (benefits, time off)
- ❌ Many focus on the tech/tools they want to work with
- ❌ Others desire to money and career climb
- ✅ The rarest lead with desire to make **impact on the business**

## Key Quote
> "Show up like an owner, not an employee."

## How to Demonstrate It
Every answer should connect back to what you will deliver, what problem you will solve, what the business will gain.
