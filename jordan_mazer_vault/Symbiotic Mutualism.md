# Symbiotic Mutualism

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s framework for the employee-employer relationship.

## The Analogy
**Clownfish and anemone.**
The anemone doesn't particularly care about the specific clownfish. But it protects the clownfish because it needs it.

## The Spectrum He Sees
- ✅ **Symbiotic** — both parties benefit; employee does great work; employer takes care of employee
- ❌ **Employee as parasite** — takes outsized salary without thinking about company impact
- ❌ **Employer as parasite** — traumatized employees who think all employers are out to get them

## His Position
> "I'm ride or die for the companies I work for. Not because I'm obsequious — because I work for companies that are ride or die for me."

## For Your Interview
Do NOT show any cynicism or chip-on-shoulder about past employers.
