# LinkedIn URL Hack

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s trick to see jobs posted in the last hour.

## How It Works
1. Search for relevant jobs on LinkedIn
2. Filter to "Past 24 Hours"
3. Find `86400` in the URL
4. Replace `86400` with `3600`
5. Go to All Filters → Sort by "Most Recent"

Result: Jobs posted in the **last hour**.

## Note
Requires turning off LinkedIn's new beta AI search.

## Why This Matters
First applicant advantage → less competition.
