# Great Managers

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s highest-value career asset: a manager who is both caring AND critical.

## The Formula
> "There's nothing more valuable at work than a manager who:
> a) cares about you
> b) believes in you  
> c) gives you deeply critical (and earned) feedback."
> Combo of 'wants you to succeed' and 'doesn't accept anything but your best' = turbo fuel.

## His Example: [[Eric Danker]]
- First manager who could give Jordan critical feedback without defensiveness
- Jordan felt Danker cared about Jordan the person, not just Jordan the employee
- Result: 4 promotions at Riot Games
- Those promotions compounded to where he is today
