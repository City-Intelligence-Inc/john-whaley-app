# Q: Why Are You Leaving Your Job?

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s advice from [[Interview Tips]].

## How to Answer
- **Do NOT answer this directly**
- Pivot immediately to what you're running **toward**
- Any negativity about your current employer = BAD

## The Reframe
Turn "leaving X" into "excited about Y."
Focus entirely on the opportunity in front of you.
