# Dailies Habit

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s productivity framework borrowed from MMO gaming.

## The Concept
"Dailies" = ritualized daily routines that keep you engaged and productive (from MMOs like WoW).

## His Personal Dailies
1. Write and publish LinkedIn post **before 7:30am**
2. Scrape inbounds and message everyone he's interested in meeting
3. Do all outreach **before reading any email or Slack**

## The History
- Agency: 50 cold calls per day or you're fired — hated it but it worked
- Amazon: imposed the rule on himself with no external pressure
- 13 straight years of some version of candidate outreach dailies

## The Insight
> "Never stop doing your dailies when your pipeline is flush."
> Consistency in inputs creates consistency in outputs.
