# Unposted Jobs

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s core insight about the hidden job market.

## The Reality
> "LinkedIn job posts are expensive and startups often don't have a ton of money."
> Most startup jobs never get posted publicly.

## Why It Matters
- The jobs that DO get posted = more competition
- The jobs that don't = require network or referral access
- This is the entire value prop of the [[Talent Network]]

## How to Access Unposted Jobs
1. [[Talent Network]] — a16z speedrun intros
2. [[ATS Site Search]] — find before they're shared
3. [[LinkedIn URL Hack]] — find the freshest postings
4. VC job boards — direct access to portcos
5. Cold outreach to founders
