# Q: How Do You Manage Priorities?

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s advice from [[Interview Tips]].

## How to Answer
- Partner with your manager and key stakeholders to establish what actually matters
- Separate **urgency** from **real impact** — they're not the same thing
- Have a concrete story ready about something you **consciously moved down the list**

## The Story Structure
Pick a real example where you:
1. Got clarity on priorities from stakeholders
2. Identified something that felt urgent but wasn't high-impact
3. Made the deliberate call to deprioritize it
4. Explain the outcome
