# Signal 3: Positive Mindset

Part of [[5 Candidate Signals]] by [[Jordan Mazer]].

## What It Means
- Stay positive throughout the interview
- Do NOT denigrate past employers, colleagues, or companies
- "Interviews are not therapy sessions, but some treat them that way"

## The Hard Rule
> "Not ready to be positive? Don't interview."

## Related to [[Hard Rules]]
Negativity about past employers is one of Jordan's absolute dealbreakers.

## How to Demonstrate It
Frame your entire job history positively. Even if your last role was toxic, talk about what you learned and what you're excited to move toward.
