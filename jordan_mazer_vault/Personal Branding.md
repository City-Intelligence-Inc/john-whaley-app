# Personal Branding

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s approach to LinkedIn content. Built 330k+ followers with a team of 3.

## Core Strategy
- [[OLAD Strategy]] — One Line A Day, every day before 7:30am
- [[Authenticity Over Polish]] — real > perfect
- Engagement posts (humor/memes) = deposits for future reach
- Value posts (job lists, tips) = what builds followers

## Tactics That Work (his data)
- 43 of his top 50 posts had an image
- Most had a **human face**
- Slightly illegible images create dwell time → better reach
- First sentence drives ~90% of post success
- Absolute positions generate engagement
- The ❤️‍🔥 emoji "absolutely dominates"

## What He Advises
> "Swallow your pride. Be willing to fail, or look dumb, or be different."
> "Most people are not competing because they don't want to look cringe."

## Results
- 330k+ followers
- 100M+ impressions targeted at job seekers
- 70k+ newsletter subscribers
- Millions of application clicks to portcos per year
