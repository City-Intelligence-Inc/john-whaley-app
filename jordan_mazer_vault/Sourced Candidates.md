# Sourced Candidates

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s nuanced view: teams that source candidates must also sell them.

## His Point
> "If you solicit interest, it's on you to get them excited throughout the vetting process."
> Starting by expecting passion from a sourced candidate is "just dumb."

## The Tension
- Inbound = wants the job → easier to sell on the role
- Sourced = content where they are → hiring team must do more work
