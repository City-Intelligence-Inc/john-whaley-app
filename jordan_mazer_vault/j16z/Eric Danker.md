# Eric Danker

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s most influential manager. Met at [[Riot Games]].

## Why He Mattered
- First manager Jordan couldn't steamroll or get defensive with
- Gave critical feedback Jordan could actually receive
- Made Jordan feel he cared about Jordan's life, not just his work output
- Took risks by promoting Jordan into successively larger roles
- Jordan felt obligation to repay that trust with performance

## The Lesson
> "The people who change your trajectory are often the ones who never know they did."
