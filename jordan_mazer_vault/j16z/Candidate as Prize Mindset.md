# Candidate as Prize Mindset

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s core interview philosophy for job seekers.

## The Idea
You are evaluating them as much as they are evaluating you.
Walk in knowing you are a valuable person who deserves a great role.

## What This Looks Like
- Asking real questions
- Being willing to walk away if it's not right
- Not being overly deferential or desperate
- Having alternatives (or at minimum your current job)

## What It Doesn't Mean
- Arrogance
- Being difficult
- Refusing to sell yourself
