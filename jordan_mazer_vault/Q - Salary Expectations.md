# Q: What Are Your Salary Expectations?

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s advice from [[Interview Tips]].

## The Rule
> **No number. Full stop.**

## What to Say
> "I'd rather understand the complete opportunity before we get into comp."

## Why
- This is your negotiating leverage
- Don't burn it in a screening call
- Information asymmetry is your friend

## Negotiation Tips (from Jordan's posts)
- Don't be the decision maker — use a partner/spouse as reason to think it over
- Have alternatives (or at minimum your current job)
- Explain WHY you need what you need (mortgage, kids, etc) — it humanizes the ask
- Always stay positive even when declining
