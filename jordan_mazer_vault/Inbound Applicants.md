# Inbound Applicants

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s most consistent public opinion: hiring teams wrongly dismiss candidates who apply.

## His Argument
- Applicants actually **want the job** — more likely to appreciate it and succeed
- Poached candidates often walk in believing they're the prize
- "It's some sort of psychological dysfunction"
- 15 years in — still watching great candidates get dismissed for "having the audacity to apply"

## Key Quote
> "The best candidate in your pipeline could very well be the one actively looking. You're just too biased to see it."

## For Your Interview
- Do NOT imply you only respond to recruiter outreach
- Framing yourself as someone who went after the opportunity will land well with him
