# Signal 1: Self-Awareness

Part of [[5 Candidate Signals]] by [[Jordan Mazer]].

## What It Means
Can you clearly articulate:
- What you **like** doing
- What you **don't** like doing
- What you **want next**
- What you're **good at**
- What you're **bad at**

## Why Most People Fail This
> "Most struggle with this because they haven't reflected."

## How to Demonstrate It
Be specific and concrete. Don't give generic "I'm a people person" answers. Know your actual strengths AND weaknesses with evidence.
