# Q: Tell Me About Yourself

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s advice from [[Interview Tips]].

## How to Answer
- **60-90 seconds max**
- Treat it like a highlight reel, not a memoir
- Hit each role briefly and keep moving
- Hobbies barely belong here (if at all)
- Family, health, religion: leave them at the door

## What to Avoid
- Going longer than 90 seconds
- Telling your whole life story
- Leading with personal details
