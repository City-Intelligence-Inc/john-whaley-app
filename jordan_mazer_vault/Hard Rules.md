# Hard Rules

Things [[Jordan Mazer]] considers dealbreakers or strong negative signals.

## Absolute No's
| Behavior | His Reaction |
|---|---|
| Camera off in video interview | "No from me dog" |
| No-show with no notice | Likely no reschedule |
| Cancel via Calendly mid-call without message | "More rude than a no-show" |
| Any negativity about past employer | Immediate negative signal |
| Giving salary number first | Burns your negotiating leverage |
| Lying in interview (e.g. claiming to speak French) | "Fatal. Also a funny memory." |

## Strong Negative Signals  
- Joining call 25 minutes late and pretending "we must have missed each other"
- Listing certifications in education section to distract from actual degree
- Using AI to cheat on technical assessments (he flags 2 in 3 candidates)
- Sending a one-line "hello" on Slack with no context ("work violence")
