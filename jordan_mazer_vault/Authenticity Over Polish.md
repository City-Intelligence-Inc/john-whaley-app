# Authenticity Over Polish

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s core content principle — and general life principle.

## In Content
- Don't make anything look perfect
- Put your face in the frame
- Say what you actually think
- Hand-written signs > professional graphics
- Slightly illegible images create dwell time

## In Life/Career
- Wears MLB shades in profile photo
- "I really do not fit the VC mold"
- Non-elite college, first job at Quiznos
- "Cringe" sign photos that colleagues find embarrassing

## The Point
> "The cringe stuff works."
> "WE ARE HERE TO GET RESULTS."

## For Your Interview
Match this energy. Don't be overly polished or performative.
He will see through it and it will hurt you.
