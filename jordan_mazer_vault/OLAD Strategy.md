# OLAD Strategy

**One Line A Day** — [[Jordan Mazer]]'s content philosophy, encouraged by [[Andrew Chen]].

## The Rule
Post **something** every single day. Anything. Don't overthink it.
Must be published **before 7:30am**.
He has done this for **600+ consecutive days**.

## Why It Works
> "If you are active, you are only competing with other highly active users — which is a fraction of the population."
> "If you rely on search, you're competing with everyone on the platform."

## What He Posts
- Job lists (drive engagement + followers)
- Hiring opinions (strong takes = comments = reach)
- Memes and humor (engagement deposits)
- Polls (prime the pump for future posts)
- Personal stories (authenticity = resonance)

## The Compound Effect
After 3 years: 330k followers, 1M+ appearances per week from posts+comments alone.
