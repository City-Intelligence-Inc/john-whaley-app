# 4 Interview Rule

[[Jordan Mazer]] cites Google's internal research finding.

## The Finding
- 4 interviews → **86% confidence** in hiring outcome
- Each additional interview adds < 1% utility
- Diminishing returns are steep after 4

## What More Than 4 Signals
1. **Role clarity issues** — they don't know what they're looking for
2. **Poor interview skills** — can't evaluate effectively
3. **Inmates run the asylum** — hiring decision outsourced to team (most common at startups)
4. **Warring silos** — most serious; org dysfunction unlikely to change
