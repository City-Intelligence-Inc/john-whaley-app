# Ship Fast

[[Jordan Mazer]]'s philosophy on execution vs deliberation.

## His View
> "People spend endless time debating assumptions rather than just shipping."
> "Sometimes they tell themselves it's 'better to wait' but they're protecting their ego."

## The Math
- Ship 10 iterations before your peers agree on their first approach
- Even if first attempt looks bad → looks much better than everyone else at version 10
- "Failed posts by definition won't be seen by a lot of people"

## Where He Applies It
- LinkedIn content (post every day, don't overthink)
- [[Vibe Coding]] (built hosted projects in days)
- Recruiting processes
